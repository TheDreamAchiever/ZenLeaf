✦ Questions

Everything You
Need to Know.

Are ZEN products safe for all skin types?

+

Yes. All ZEN products are formulated for all skin types including sensitive and reactive skin. We never use synthetic fragrance, sulphates, parabens, or any known irritants. Each formula is tested on sensitive skin volunteers before release.

How long before I see results?

+

Most customers report visible improvement in skin texture and glow within 7–14 days of consistent use. Full transformational results are typically seen within 21–30 days. Nature works at her own perfect pace.

Are your products vegan and cruelty-free?

+

Absolutely. Every ZEN product is 100% vegan, cruelty-free, and certified by the Vegan Society. We have never and will never test on animals. Our ethics are non-negotiable.

What is your shipping and return policy?

+

We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹999 across India. International shipping is available to 40+ countries. We accept hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery — no questions asked.

How are ingredients sourced and verified?

+

Every ingredient is wild-harvested by trained foragers who work directly with tribal and farming communities. Each batch is tested for purity, potency, and contamination before it enters our blending room. Full traceability reports are available on request.

Can I use ZEN products during pregnancy?

+

While all our ingredients are natural, we recommend consulting your healthcare provider before beginning any new skincare routine during pregnancy. We are happy to share full ingredient lists so your doctor can advise accordingly.
